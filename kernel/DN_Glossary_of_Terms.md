# Dimensional Glossary for the DN Framework & Growth Blueprint
This glossary is designed for Facilitators, AI model trainers, and
strategic practitioners using the DN Framework. Use it to align language
across documents, enable simulation consistency, and deepen dimensional
fluency across teams and systems.

The following harmonized nomenclature for the **DN Framework** and the
**Growth Blueprint**, includes both standard (practical) and poetic
(philosophical/metaphorical) interpretations of each term. It is
designed to support coherence across documents and deepen applied
understanding through multidimensional framing.

**Each entry contains:**

-   **Standard Term**: The term to be used consistently in formal or
structured usage

-   **Poetic Synonyms**: Alternate, symbolic, or intuitive framings used
in different documents or sessions

-   **Definition**: Clear explanation suitable for practical application

-   **Core Pillar Breakdown**: Understanding each concept through
Heart/Truth/Nuance trinity

-   **Application**: Included to support flexible yet coherent usage

**Vocabulary note:** This Glossary uses Growth Blueprint application-layer terminology (artifact, bridge, board, zone, exercise, snapshot, workspace) alongside the DN Kernel's universal terms (Signal, Link, Field, Region, Process, Capture, Container). The complete mapping is documented in the Kernel's §0.4 vocabulary table. Where application-layer terms appear in definitions below, the corresponding kernel term is noted parenthetically on first use within each entry. Kernel terms are not re-listed as separate Glossary entries to avoid version drift between documents.

## Core Pillars (Trinity Framework)
### Heart
*Poetic Synonyms:* Spark, Soul, Essence, Flame, Pulse

**Definition:** The emotional and motivational core of intelligence.
Heart represents purpose, desire, authenticity, and alignment with inner
truth. It is not sentimentality—it is the source of conviction and the
energy behind action.

**Heart:** Activates inspiration and vision. The "why" behind a
decision.
**Truth:** Must be paired with evidence to avoid blind passion.
**Nuance:** Embraces complexity of emotion, identity, and value systems.

**Application:**

-   Start with Heart when anchoring vision or aligning a team.

-   Ask, "What do we care about here? Why does this matter beyond
success?"

-   Use to check for resonance in simulations and strategy development.

### Truth
*Poetic Synonyms:* Fire, Mirror, Signal, Foundation

**Definition:** The layer of validated information and structural
integrity. Truth gives form to intuition through evidence, coherence,
clarity, and logical progression. It stabilizes the wildness of
potential with structure.

**Heart:** Keeps passion honest and ethically aligned.
**Truth:** Offers reliability, measurability, and replicability.
**Nuance:** Holds the tension between certainty and complexity.

**Application:**

-   Use Truth when grounding abstract ideas or resolving contradictions.

-   Ask, "What can be tested here? What's the signal behind the noise?"

-   Vital during Fire Gates, Signal Lock moments, and scenario
validations.

### Nuance
*Poetic Synonyms:* Wind, Context, Layer, Prism

**Definition:** The connective intelligence that reveals depth, pattern,
interdependence, and contradiction. Nuance avoids black-and-white
thinking and opens space for empathy, timing, and complexity.

**Heart:** Allows space for emotional paradox and complexity.
**Truth:** Prevents binary assumptions by mapping multiple
perspectives.
**Nuance:** Enables dimensional bridging, synthesis, and coherence over
simplicity.

**Application:**

-   Bring in Nuance when facing tension, disagreement, or systems-level
challenges.

-   Ask, "What aren't we seeing? What assumptions are we operating
inside?"

-   Essential for Dimensional Tuning, Context Weaving, and
scenario-layering prompts.
