How to See

You Are the Light

Your cursor is your attention. You are inside a field of nine dimensions. Move, and dimensions reveal. Pull away, and entropy reclaims the dark.

Click — Signal Lock / Shadow Break

In light mode: Click near an illuminated signal to Signal Lock it — a conscious attestation of convergence. Locked signals become gravitational anchors pulling nearby intelligence into orbit.

In shadow mode: Click triggers a Shadow Break — an explosive separation event. Nearby signals are violently repelled. The anti-lock.

Keys

S Shadow Mode — Attention creates darkness. What you see vanishes. What you ignore reveals.

T Story Threads — The seven archetypal arcs connecting dimensions. Threads glow when both endpoints are illuminated.

P Parallax — Toggle 3D depth. When on, your cursor shifts the field layers. When off, only your light moves.

Sliders

Immersion ↔ Awareness: Dissolved in the field vs. seeing its structure.

Spectrum ↔ Focus: Wide soft beam vs. tight intense beam.

Ambient ↔ Directed: Self-glowing field vs. only-where-you-aim.

Resonance: How readily signals bind to compatible neighbors.

Frontier Chaos: How much 9D energy enters from the unknown edges.

Dimensional Personalities

1D Spark — Flickers urgently. Raw impulse. Can ignite nearby signals.
2D Reaction — Snaps between states. Tests neighbors with brief flashing links.
3D Context — Forms triangular clusters. Widest awareness radius.
4D Temporal — Long memory trails. Occasionally reverses, reading time.
5D Singularity — Strongest gravity. Attracts from ANY dimension. The inflection point.
6D Connection — Bridges across 4+ dimensions. Longest reach. The love bridge.
7D Manifestation — Pulses brightly. Clusters crystallize into stable forms and birth new signals.
8D Recursion — Orbits gravity centers. Spirals inward. Self-referential paths.
9D Frontier — Enters from the edges. Phases in and out. Unpredictable.

In Shadow Mode

Each dimension inverts its behavior. 1D repels passionately. 5D consumes rather than attracts. 6D extracts rather than bridges. 7D cages rather than creates. 8D spirals tighter toward destruction. 9D circles inward rather than arriving.

The Signal Bands

You may notice entire dimensional bands "exciting" at once — horizontal streaks of light shooting across the field. This is Resonant Activation (Kernel Constraint 5): when multiple signals in the same dimensional band are illuminated simultaneously, the trail persistence creates a visible harmonic. You're seeing the field resonate at a specific dimensional frequency.
