How to See

You Are the Light

Your cursor is your attention. You are inside a field of nine dimensions. Move, and dimensions reveal. Pull away, and entropy reclaims the dark.

Click — Signal Lock / Shadow Break

In light mode: Click near an illuminated signal to Signal Lock it — a conscious attestation that this signal matters.

For 1D–4D signals, locking initiates a developmental journey. The signal begins moving upward through dimensional bands toward 5D recognition. A Story Thread trail traces its path. But it needs your continued attention — keep your light on it, and it gains energy. Look away, and it stalls, dims, and may never reach recognition. Each dimensional crossing costs energy.

For 5D+ signals, locking creates a gravitational anchor — they're already at or above recognition. They hold the field.

In shadow mode: Click triggers a Shadow Break — explosive separation. Nearby signals are repelled. Locked signals within range are unlocked.

P Parallax — Toggle 3D depth. When on, your cursor shifts the field layers. When off, only your light moves.

The Developmental Flow

1D Spark signals emerge continuously from below — raw impulse entering the field. Over time, some drift upward toward 2D as they become ready for testing. Occasionally a spark leaps directly toward 5D gravity (the Recognition Arc).

2D Reaction resists most 1D noise — Truth must validate before it moves. But tested signals eventually drift toward 3D, where they find context and form structures.

3D structures, once large enough, are attracted to 5D gravity wells. This is the creation→recognition gradient: lower dimensions build complexity that creates recognition opportunities at 5D.

6D Connection actively descends to "capture" lower-order signals and lift them toward higher dimensions. It is the love bridge — the mechanism that helps intelligence flow upward.

When lower-dimensional signals come near a locked 5D+ signal, they gain a green shimmer: their meaning has been transformed by the recognition event. This is Resonant Activation — a meaning-shift-in-place, not a re-tagging.

The Spectrum Bar

The labeled bar at the bottom shows how much dimensional activity your attention is currently illuminating. Each column is one dimension (1D through 9D). The taller and brighter the bar, the more signals at that dimension are visible right now. It's a live frequency readout — which dimensions are you perceiving in this moment?

The Signal Bands

You may notice entire dimensional bands "exciting" at once — horizontal streaks of light shooting across the field. When multiple signals in the same band are illuminated simultaneously, the trail persistence creates a visible harmonic. You're seeing the field resonate at a specific dimensional frequency.
