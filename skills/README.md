# DN Skills

DN skills are reusable methodology files that bring the Framework's structural thinking into AI-assisted work. Each skill is self-contained and grounded in the canonical [DN Kernel](../kernel/DN_Kernel.md) and [Glossary](../kernel/DN_Glossary_of_Terms.md).

They are not autonomous agents. Provide the relevant skill file to an AI system as instructions or context, then bring it the subject you want to explore.

## DN Idea Cartographer

The Idea Cartographer is the universal interpretation layer: a source-agnostic process for mapping any subject through DN without using the Framework as a proving ground.

It supports four depths:

- **Sketch** — rapid orientation for a premise or early idea
- **Survey** — a complete nine-dimensional profile of a developed work
- **Deep Field** — evidence-grounded analysis with alternate interpretations
- **Atlas** — recursive mapping of a series, corpus, organization, or multi-part system

Its central distinction is that **coordinates can be universal, while scores require a declared destination**. Dimensional expression, evidentiary confidence, source coverage, pillars, RIP, field topology, shadow, and Open Horizon remain separately visible. This makes radically different subjects comparable without allowing length, polish, or volume to become proxies for completeness or worth.

## How the Skills Relate

The [`DN Idea Cartographer`](SKILL_DN-Idea-Cartographer.md) maps the terrain. The [`Growth Blueprint`](SKILL_Growth-Blueprint.md) applies dimensional intelligence to organizational strategy. [`Pre-Session Intel`](SKILL_Pre-Session-Intel.md) gathers and positions source signals for that strategic work. [`DN Concept to Canvas`](SKILL_DN-Concept-to-Canvas.md) translates conceptual dynamics into an interactive visual experience.

Use them independently or as complementary layers. The Kernel and Glossary remain the structural source of truth.

## Using a Skill

1. Open or download the skill's Markdown file.
2. Provide it to an AI system as instructions or context.
3. State the subject, source boundary, and intended outcome.
4. Let the skill establish any additional mapping or facilitation parameters.
5. Retain human authority over Signal Lock, interpretation, and consequential choices.
